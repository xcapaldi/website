I am a Ph.D. candidate at McGill University working with Professor Walter Reisner. Our research is focused on the behavior of nanoconfined polymers and how such systems can model polymer confinement in biological systems. We use nanofabrication and nanofluidics as tools to confine, manipulate and study DNA, which is our biological polymer of interest. This work is valuable both to fundamental physics as well as genetic sequencing techniques.
