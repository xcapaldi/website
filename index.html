Xavier Capaldi

I'm a Ph.D. candidate in the biological and biomedical engineering program at McGill University (Montréal, Québec). I work with Professor Walter Reisner in the department of physics. My M.Sc. in physics was completed under his tutelage on the topic of nanoconfined polymers. I developed and fabricated nanofluidic devices with pneumatically-actuated membranes capable of trapping multiple DNA molecules in nanoscale cavities. Now my Ph.D. work has shifted toward solid-state nanopore sequencing technologies. You can see my publications and projects listed below.
